About Us

At Sahi Daftar, we take pride in being a trusted name in licensing and regulatory services, with over 10 years of proven expertise in the industry. Our mission is to simplify complex licensing procedures for individuals, startups, and established businesses, ensuring a smooth and reliable experience from start to finish.

With a team of highly qualified professionals, we specialize in delivering end-to-end support — from preparing documentation to coordinating with authorities — until the license is successfully issued. We are committed to accuracy, transparency, and timeliness in every assignment we undertake.

Over the past decade, Sahi Daftar has built a reputation for excellence, not only by providing quality services but also by ensuring complete peace of mind to our clients. We understand that every license is critical to the growth and credibility of your business, and therefore we dedicate ourselves to handling every process with utmost care, confidentiality, and efficiency.

Our approach is client-centric:

  • We guide and convince our clients with absolute clarity at every stage.
  • We take complete ownership of the process until the final license is delivered.
  • We ensure timely updates and regular communication.
  • We emphasize accuracy in documentation and compliance with all legal requirements.

Whether you are an entrepreneur, a growing organization, or an established enterprise, Sahi Daftar is your reliable partner for all licensing needs. With us, you don’t just get a service provider; you gain a long-term partner dedicated to ensuring that your compliance requirements are met seamlessly and professionally.

Services we offer

Comprehensive compliance services tailored to your stage and sector.

ITR & Tax Filing

Accurate income tax return filing for individuals and businesses, including TDS/TCS and audit support.

  • Individual & corporate ITR
  • TDS returns
  • Tax audit coordination

GST Registration & Returns

End-to-end GST services: registration, monthly/quarterly filings, reconciliation, and notices.

  • GSTIN registration
  • GSTR-1, 3B, 9/9C
  • ITC reconciliation

Company Incorporation

Private Ltd, LLP, OPC setup with DSC, DIN, and MoA/AoA drafting and MCA filings.

  • Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC
  • DIN & DSC
  • Post-incorporation compliance

ROC & Corporate Compliance

Annual filings, board and shareholder documentation, and event-based compliance.

  • AOC-4, MGT-7/7A
  • Board minutes & registers
  • Change in directors/office

Payroll & Labour Laws

Payroll setup, PF/ESI/PT, Shops & Establishments, and POSH compliance.

  • PF, ESI, PT registrations
  • Monthly challans & returns
  • Policy drafting & audits

Trademark & Legal Advisory

Trademark search, filing, replies, and general contracts and legal opinions.

  • TM search & filing
  • Objection replies
  • Contracts & policies
