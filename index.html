Services we offer

Comprehensive compliance services tailored to your stage and sector.

ITR & Tax Filing

Accurate income tax return filing for individuals and businesses, including TDS/TCS and audit support.

  • Individual & corporate ITR
  • TDS returns
  • Tax audit coordination

GST Registration & Returns

End-to-end GST services: registration, monthly/quarterly filings, reconciliation, and notices.

  • GSTIN registration
  • GSTR-1, 3B, 9/9C
  • ITC reconciliation

Company Incorporation

Private Ltd, LLP, OPC setup with DSC, DIN, and MoA/AoA drafting and MCA filings.

  • Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC
  • DIN & DSC
  • Post-incorporation compliance

ROC & Corporate Compliance

Annual filings, board and shareholder documentation, and event-based compliance.

  • AOC-4, MGT-7/7A
  • Board minutes & registers
  • Change in directors/office

Payroll & Labour Laws

Payroll setup, PF/ESI/PT, Shops & Establishments, and POSH compliance.

  • PF, ESI, PT registrations
  • Monthly challans & returns
  • Policy drafting & audits

Trademark & Legal Advisory

  • TM search & filing
  • Objection replies
